How to Play the game

1. There are cards all shuffled together facing down.
2. Turn over any two cards.
3. If the two cards match, they stay open.
4. If they don't match, the cards turn back.
5. Remember what was on each card and where it was.
6. The game is over when all the cards have been matched.
